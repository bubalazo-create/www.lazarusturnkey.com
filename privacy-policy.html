Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: March 2026

At Lazarus Turnkey Contractor ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the personal information we collect from you when you use our website (www.lazarusturnkey.com).

1. Information We Collect

When you fill out our "Get a Quote" or contact form, we collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide to us:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number (if provided in your message)
  • Details about your project requested through the message field

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes for our turnkey services.
  • Communicate with you regarding your ongoing or potential projects.

3. Data Sharing and Transfer

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. Your information is only shared with our internal team and trusted partners (such as CIL Services or SMA) if it is directly necessary to execute your requested project, and only with your prior knowledge.

4. Data Retention

We retain your contact form submissions for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, typically the duration of your project and warranty period, after which it is securely deleted.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use Google Analytics to monitor website traffic and improve user experience. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data about how visitors use our site. However, we respect your privacy: these tracking cookies are only activated if you explicitly click "Accept" on our cookie consent banner. If you decline, no tracking cookies are placed on your device.

We also use standard external libraries (like Google Fonts and FontAwesome), which may log your IP address temporarily on their respective servers to serve files, but they do not store user-tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.
