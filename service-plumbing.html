Reliable Plumbing Installations in Malta

Plumbing is one of the most critical elements of any finishing project. Our certified plumbers lay all hot and cold water supply lines, install drainage and waste systems, and connect all sanitary fixtures. We coordinate closely with electricians and tilers to ensure everything is in place before walls and floors are closed.

From a single apartment bathroom to a full villa fit-out with multiple bathrooms, kitchen, laundry and outdoor supply points, we handle it all.

What Our Plumbing Service Includes

  • Hot and cold water supply pipe routing
  • Drainage and waste pipe installation
  • Shower tray and bath installation
  • Toilet, basin and bidet connection
  • Boiler and water heater connections
  • Kitchen sink and dishwasher supply
  • Washing machine and tumble dryer connections
  • Outdoor tap and garden irrigation points
  • Water pressure testing and commissioning

Materials We Use

  • PPR and copper pipe for hot and cold supply
  • PVC and soil pipe for waste drainage
  • Brass isolation valves and fittings
  • Flexible hoses for fixture connections
  • Insulation for hot water pipes to prevent heat loss
  • Pressure-tested compression fittings

Why It Matters

All plumbing must be completed and pressure-tested before tiling and waterproofing. A leak discovered after tiles are laid means breaking out finished work — costing time and money. Our systematic approach ensures all pipes are correctly routed, supported and tested before any surface finishes are applied.
