Facade & Exterior Finishing in Malta

The exterior of your property is the first thing people see. Our facade finishing service covers external rendering, insulated facade systems (ETICS), stone cladding, feature panels and exterior painting — giving your building a clean, distinctive and weather-resistant finish.

We work on both residential villas and apartment blocks, providing durable systems that stand up to Malta's hot summers, sea air and winter rain.

What Our Facade Service Includes

  • Sand-cement external render
  • Thin-coat silicone and mineral render finishes
  • External insulation and finishing system (ETICS / EWI)
  • Natural and engineered stone cladding
  • Feature panel installation (aluminium, ACM, composite)
  • External tile cladding
  • Sealant and expansion joint application
  • Balcony and parapet wall finishing
  • External painting

Materials We Use

  • Weber, Sto or Baumit thin-coat render systems
  • EPS and mineral wool insulation boards for ETICS
  • Fibreglass mesh reinforcement for render coats
  • Silicone-based top coats for hydrophobic protection
  • External grade adhesive for stone and tile cladding
  • Polyurethane and silicone expansion joint sealants

Why It Matters

Malta's climate is demanding on exterior surfaces. Poor render cracks and allows moisture ingress, leading to damp, efflorescence and structural damage. The right system applied correctly — with proper mesh reinforcement, primer coats and joint detailing — lasts decades and maintains its appearance with minimal maintenance.
