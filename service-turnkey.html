Full Turnkey Finishing in Malta

Our turnkey service is the complete package — one contractor, one point of contact, every trade handled. From shell apartment to fully finished home, we manage the entire programme. You deal with us; we coordinate electricians, plumbers, plasterers, tilers and painters.

Our Process

1
Survey & Quotation — We visit, assess and produce a detailed itemised quote.
2
Electrical & Plumbing Rough-in — All conduits and pipes installed before walls close.
3
Waterproofing & Screed — Wet areas sealed and tested; floors levelled.
4
Plastering & Gypsum — Walls plastered; ceilings and partitions built.
5
Tiling & Flooring — All floor and wall tiles laid and grouted.
6
Painting — Walls, ceilings and woodwork finished.
7
Second Fix & Commissioning — Fixtures, sanitary ware, lights installed.
8
Handover — Clean, finished and ready to occupy.

What Is Included

  • Complete electrical installation
  • Complete plumbing systems
  • Internal plastering and external render
  • Gypsum ceilings and partitions
  • Waterproofing of all wet areas
  • Floor screeding
  • Full tiling (floors, walls, bathrooms)
  • Painting interior and exterior
  • Bathroom installation
  • Project management and coordination
