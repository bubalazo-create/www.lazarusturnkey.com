Complete Bathroom Installation in Malta

We install bathrooms from scratch — from the first plumbing rough-in right through to hanging towel rails and installing mirrors. Our team coordinates waterproofing, tiling, plumbing connections and electrical work to deliver a fully functional, beautifully finished bathroom.

Whether it is a compact en-suite or a spacious master bathroom with a free-standing bath, walk-in shower and double vanity, we handle every detail.

What Our Bathroom Installation Includes

  • Waterproofing of shower areas and wet zones
  • Floor and wall tiling
  • Shower tray or walk-in wet room installation
  • Bath installation (built-in or free-standing)
  • Toilet and bidet fitting
  • Vanity unit and basin connection
  • Shower screen and door fitting
  • Towel rail, mirror and accessories installation
  • Extractor fan wiring and fitting
  • Heated towel rail connection

Materials & Fixtures We Work With

  • All major sanitary ware brands (Grohe, Hansgrohe, Roca, Ideal Standard)
  • Wall-hung and floor-standing WC units
  • Concealed cistern frames (Geberit, Grohe)
  • All tile types including large format wall and floor tiles
  • Glass shower screens and sliding doors
  • Chrome, brushed nickel and matte black finishes

Why It Matters

Bathroom installation requires careful sequencing — plumbing must come before tiling, waterproofing before tiling, and electrical before gypsum ceilings. Our coordinated team ensures every trade is done in the right order, with no costly mistakes or rework.
