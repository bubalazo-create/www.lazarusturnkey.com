Pool Finishing & Tiling in Malta

Swimming pool finishing requires specialist knowledge of waterproofing systems, epoxy grout and pool-grade mosaic or ceramic tiles that can withstand constant water immersion and chemical exposure. Our team has experience finishing both new pools and refurbishing existing ones.

We handle the full pool finishing sequence: waterproofing the shell, tiling the floor and walls, laying coping stones, and finishing the pool surround area.

What Our Pool Finishing Service Includes

  • Pool shell waterproofing (cement-based and membrane systems)
  • Mosaic tile installation on pool floor and walls
  • Ceramic and porcelain pool tile installation
  • Waterline tile band installation
  • Coping stone and edge tile fitting
  • Pool surround and deck tiling
  • Epoxy grouting for long-term chemical resistance
  • Skimmer and light recess sealing

Materials We Use

  • Pool-grade mosaic tiles (glass, ceramic, porcelain)
  • Epoxy tile adhesive for submerged surfaces
  • Two-component epoxy grout (Mapei Kerapoxy, Litokol Starlike)
  • Cementitious waterproofing slurry for shell preparation
  • Silicone movement joint sealant at coping level
  • Non-slip porcelain for pool surround areas

Why It Matters

Standard tile adhesives and grouts fail in pools — the constant water pressure, chemical exposure and UV light degrade ordinary materials. Only products specifically rated for pool use will last. We use the correct materials and apply them to manufacturer specifications, including primer coats, correct adhesive coverage and full epoxy grout joints that resist algae and chemical attack.
