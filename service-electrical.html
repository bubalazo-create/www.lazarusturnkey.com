Professional Electrical Installations in Malta

At Lazarus Turnkey Contractor, our certified electricians handle every aspect of electrical work — from the initial rough-in wiring on a shell property to the final connection of fixtures and smart home systems. We work on both residential apartments and villas, ensuring all installations comply with Maltese building regulations.

Whether you are finishing a new apartment from scratch or upgrading an existing property, we provide a full electrical service that integrates safely and neatly into your finishes.

What Our Electrical Service Includes

  • Full distribution board installation and labelling
  • In-wall conduit and cable routing
  • Socket, switch and USB outlet installation
  • Recessed and surface-mounted lighting
  • Underfloor heating wiring
  • Smart home wiring (dimmers, automation, control panels)
  • Air conditioning pre-wiring
  • Doorbell, intercom and CCTV conduit preparation
  • External socket and garden lighting
  • Earthing and bonding

Materials We Use

  • Copper conductors (2.5mm², 4mm², 6mm² depending on circuit)
  • PVC and corrugated conduit for cable protection
  • RCCB / MCB distribution boards (Schneider, Hager or equivalent)
  • Flush-mounted back boxes (metal and plastic)
  • Premium branded sockets and switches (Bticino, Simon, ABB)
  • LED-compatible downlight fittings

Why It Matters

Electrical work must be done before plastering and tiling. Poor wiring can create safety hazards and costly repairs later. Our team plans the full electrical layout in advance, coordinating with plumbers and plasterers so every chase, conduit and box is in the right place before walls are closed up.
