Professional Painting Services in Malta

Painting is the final transformation — it gives personality and depth to a space. Our painters work on properly plastered and primed surfaces, applying paint in clean, even coats that hold their colour and finish for years. We work with all major paint brands and can mix any colour to match your design specification.

We cover internal walls and ceilings, external facades, feature walls, woodwork, metal railings and more.

What Our Painting Service Includes

  • Interior wall and ceiling painting
  • External facade painting and rendering sealing
  • Feature wall painting and colour blocking
  • Door and window frame painting
  • Skirting board and architrave painting
  • Metal railing and gate painting
  • Primer and undercoat application
  • Sanding and gap filling before paint
  • Custom colour matching

Materials We Use

  • Premium interior emulsions (Dulux, Tikkurila or equivalent)
  • Exterior masonry paint with weather resistance
  • High-build primer for new plaster surfaces
  • Oil-based gloss and satinwood for woodwork
  • Anti-mould paint for bathrooms and humid areas
  • Masking tape and dust sheets for clean edges and floors

Why It Matters

Painting is always the last stage — done after plastering, gypsum work, tiling and joinery are complete. Any paint applied too early risks damage from other trades. Our team schedules painting correctly, works on clean prepared surfaces and protects all finished floors and fittings before starting.
